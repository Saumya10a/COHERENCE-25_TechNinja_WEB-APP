W.A.V.E

W.A.V.E is a comprehensive smart city dashboard designed to enhance urban living through real-time data aggregation and intelligent insights. By collecting data from open APIs and user inputs, it provides a dynamic visualization of critical city parameters such as traffic flow, air quality, water levels, and energy consumption. Leveraging AI-driven analytics, WAVE predicts trends, generates proactive alerts, and empowers authorities and citizens to make informed decisions. With its intuitive interface and mobile-friendly design, WAVE ensures seamless access to actionable insights, fostering a more efficient, sustainable, and responsive urban environment.
